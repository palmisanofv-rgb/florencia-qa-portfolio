# 00 — QA Strategy & Leadership

The other 10 projects in this portfolio are execution: hands-on testing across 10 domains and 7 tools. This folder is the layer above that — the thinking a **QA Lead/Manager** applies to turn 10 individual projects into one coherent quality program: why each tool was chosen, how risk gets prioritized across a team's limited time, and what gets reported upward.

## Contents

- [`test-strategy-master.md`](test-strategy-master.md) — the organizing philosophy behind every project in this portfolio (test pyramid, risk-based prioritization, environment strategy)
- [`tool-tech-matrix.md`](tool-tech-matrix.md) — decision framework for tool selection (why Playwright *and* Selenium *and* Katalon exist side by side, rather than picking just one)
- [`metrics-and-reporting-framework.md`](metrics-and-reporting-framework.md) — the KPIs a QA Lead would track and report to stakeholders, applied retrospectively to this portfolio's 10 projects
